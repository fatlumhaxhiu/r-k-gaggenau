# Design System Theme

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional parameters of our design system.

## Color Palette

Our color palette is designed to be harmonious and accessible, supporting a clear visual hierarchy.

*   **Color Mode**: `light`
*   **Primary Color**: `#008855` - A vibrant and commanding color, ideal for call-to-action buttons and key interactive elements.
*   **Secondary Color**: `#3399CC` - A supportive accent color that complements the primary hue, used for less prominent UI elements and chips.
*   **Neutral Color**: `#F8FAFA` - A clean, foundational color for backgrounds and non-chromatic surfaces, ensuring readability and visual comfort.

## Typography

Our typography system prioritizes legibility and brand expression across all content.

*   **Headline Font**: `Manrope` - Selected for its modern and approachable feel, perfect for strong headings and titles.
*   **Body Font**: `Inter` - A highly legible and versatile typeface, used for all long-form content and general text.
*   **Label Font**: `Inter` - Consistent with the body font, ensuring clarity and consistency for form labels and interactive elements.

## Shape and Spacing

These properties define the physical attributes and layout of our UI components.

*   **Roundedness**: `Moderate (2)` - A balanced level of roundedness applied to UI elements, providing a friendly yet professional aesthetic.
*   **Spacing**: `Normal (2)` - Standard spacing between elements, ensuring a comfortable and organized layout without feeling too dense or sparse.
